Simplify Hostel & Property Management

The all-in-one app for hostel and PG owners. Create your hostel/PG, add rooms (with auto-created beds), onboard tenants with auto-billing, manage staff, and monitor everything from your dashboard.

Everything Owners Need to Manage Their Hostel or PG

Powerful features designed for hostel and PG owners to manage operations efficiently

Property Management

Owners can create and manage their hostel or PG, set the name and address, and monitor everything from a single dashboard.

Tenant Management

Onboard tenants in 2 steps: enter tenant info (name, address, document ID, assign room and bed), then set up auto-billing (enable/disable monthly autopayment).

Room Management

Create rooms for your hostel or PG. Specify bed count and beds are auto-created in the backend. Assign tenants to specific rooms and beds.

Staff Management

Add and manage staff members, assign roles, and coordinate your team for efficient hostel operations.

Subscription Plans

Flexible pricing plans for owners, scaling with your hostel or PG business needs.

Reports & Analytics

Dashboard with detailed reports, occupancy analytics, and revenue tracking for hostel/PG owners.

How It Works for Hostel/PG Owners

Manage your hostel or property in a few easy steps

1

Create Your Hostel or PG

Add your hostel or PG details (name, address) as an owner after installing the app.

2

Create Rooms & Beds

Add rooms to your hostel or PG. Specify bed count for each room—beds are auto-created in the backend.

3

Onboard Tenants & Manage Staff

Add tenants in 2 steps: enter tenant info (name, address, document ID, assign room/bed), then set up auto-billing. Add staff and manage all operations from your dashboard.
