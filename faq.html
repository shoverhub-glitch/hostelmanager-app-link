Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Hostel Manager

What is Hostel Manager app?
Hostel Manager is an Android app that helps hostel and PG (paying guest) owners manage their properties, rooms, beds, tenants, and staff from their phone. It's designed to simplify property management tasks and automate billing.
How to manage tenants in Hostel Manager?
You can onboard tenants in just 2 steps: enter tenant information (name, address, document ID, assign room and bed), then set up auto-billing with monthly payment automation.
Is Hostel Manager free to download?
Yes, Hostel Manager is free to download from Google Play Store. It offers flexible pricing plans for additional features as your business grows.
Can I manage multiple hostels with Hostel Manager?
Yes, Hostel Manager allows you to create and manage multiple hostels or PG properties from a single dashboard. This is perfect for property owners with multiple locations.
How does auto-billing work?
Hostel Manager allows you to set up auto-billing for each tenant. You can enable or disable monthly autopayment, set payment amounts, and the app will help track and manage recurring payments automatically.
Does Hostel Manager support staff management?
Yes, Hostel Manager includes comprehensive staff management features. You can add staff members, assign roles, and coordinate your team for efficient hostel operations.
What reports and analytics are available?
Hostel Manager provides a dashboard with detailed reports, occupancy analytics, and revenue tracking to help hostel and PG owners make data-driven decisions.
How do I create rooms and beds?
Creating rooms is simple: just add a room and specify the bed count. Beds are automatically created in the backend. You can then assign tenants to specific rooms and beds.
Is my data secure?
Yes, Hostel Manager takes data security seriously. Your data is stored securely and you can request account deletion at any time.
How can I delete my account?
To delete your account, send an email to contact@shoverhub.com from your registered email address with the subject "Delete my Hostel Manager account". Your data will be permanently deleted within 7 days.
